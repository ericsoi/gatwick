    About Us

    Gatwick In-House Training Consultants firm was established in the year 2018, targeting beginers in the insurance industry, to train and instill within them sufficient job knowledge in the insurance industry that enables them to work with professionalism and confidence, not only to attract customers but also to reduce company expenses. We have an Insurance Agency that is licensed to Carry on General Insurance, Health Insurance and Life Assurance business within the Republic of Kenya. Chase Farm Insurance Agency has been licensed by Insurance Regulatory Authority and is an agent in Kenya, subject to the provision of the Insurance Act 1984.

    Employer's Personal Accident

    Contractor's All Risk

    Travel Insurance

    Travel insurance has been tailored to meet all needs of students, groups, individuals and corporate business travellers. For peace of mind in a foreign environment do not leave home without your travel guard insurance cover. It is tailored to cover accidental injuries, medical expenses, losses of personal effects and travel delays, hijack, personal liability and legal expenses etc.

    Fire & Perils Insurance

    The basic fire policy provides indemnity against loss or damage to property described, caused by fire, lightening and explosion. However, the policy can also be extended to cover riot, strike, malicious damage, storm, earthquakes, impact by vehicles, aircraft and other aerial devices, subterranean fire, spontaneous combustion, explosion of industrial boilers and economizers, flooding, water damage due to bursting or overflowing of water tanks, apparatus and pipes.

    Property Terrorism & Sabotage Insurance

    The cover has been specifically designed to cover the sabotage, terrorism, mutiny, rebellion, insurrection and coup d’états (excluding war and civil war) risks faced by multinational companies operating in countries where these risks are normally excluded standard insurance programs.     Public Liability Insurance

    Cover provides protection against claims arising from: ▪ Accidental bodily injury to any person ▪ Accidental loss of or damage to property Happening in connection with the business and occurring within the territorial limits described and happening during the period of insurance. Since the promise to indemnify is the basis of the policy, strictly speaking insurers are not liable to make payment until the insured has been found "legally liable" to pay for the damages.

    Banker's Blanket Bond Insurance

    Banks and other financial institutions face a higher level of exposure to financial loss resulting from criminal behavior than most other institutions. Employee theft, embezzlement, forgery hold up and fraud are all real risks in today’s business environment and even more so in the financial sector. The bankers blanket bond policy addresses these risks. As the name implies this is a comprehensive crime and fidelity package designed specifically for banks and other financial institutions.
